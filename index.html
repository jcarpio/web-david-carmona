ABOUT THE ALBUM

Each song in the album has its own story, its own “madness” experienced. I gained experience with each of them. I left musical ideas along the way, which gave rise to new ones. I suffered the anguish of emptiness, not create anything, be lacking ideas; and, on the contrary, I felt that “divine grace” to have found, a motive with something of interest, that I could develop. There were experiences, memories……
So, looking back, after everything worked, learned and lived I can say this was not a simple dream, this is and I am afraid it will still be:
“Un sueño de locura”
David Carmona
